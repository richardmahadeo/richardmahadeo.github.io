Cherrios

Cheerios is a cereal brand owned by General Mills that is made from whole grain oats. The brand was introduced in 1941 as CheeriOats, but the name was changed to Cheerios in 1945. Cheerios is known for its distinctive O shape and is considered a nutritious and family-friendly cereal.
